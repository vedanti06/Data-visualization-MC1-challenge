TITLE: MAGNIFICENT OPENING GASTECH-KRONOS

PUBLISHED: 1995/10/12

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

With his flash of the registered tradename, Sten Sanjorge, president of international GAStech it opened the new headquarters of GAStech-Kronos. The tape was cut with an enormous pair of the scissors, matched of size only by the smile of Sanjorge's. 

50 million  Dollars for the new headquarters are right a drop in the bucket for GAStech. The gas field in the production outside Abila has exceeded even the expectations great size of Sanjorge, having paid the building often above. 

The present in the ceremony of the cut of the tape was several representatives of the government of Kronos.
