TITLE: DOCUMENTARY CARD OF PUBLIC HEALTH Of ELODIS 2004

PUBLISHED: 2004/05/31


LOCATION: ELODIS, Kronos 

The center of health of the Community of Elodis published an update with health for all the study carried out in 1998.  The death rate and the incidence of the chronic disease increased more than 252% of the former years.  The detailed report will be announced soon on the municipal Web site of Elodis.
