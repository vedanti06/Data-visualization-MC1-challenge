TITLE: Breaking: Emergency at GAStech Headquarters Building [Updates]
AUTHOR: Cato Rossini, Marcella Trapani
PUBLISHED: 20 January 2014

Update, 11:55 PM:  Abila Fire Captain Federigo Accardi announces that it was a false alarm and the building is safe.  In response to reporter's questions, Captain Accardi says GAStech employees made no report of a bomb threat to his agency.
