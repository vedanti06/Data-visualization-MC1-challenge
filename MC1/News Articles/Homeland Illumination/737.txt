TITLE: ELODIS PUBLIC HEALTH FACT SHEET 2004
AUTHOR: Petrus Gerhard
PUBLISHED: 29 May 2004

LOCATION: ELODIS, Kronos

The Elodis Community Health Center has published an update to the Wellness for All study performed in 1998.  The mortality rate and incidence of chronic illness increased over 252% from prior years.  The full report will soon be posted on the Elodis municipal website.
