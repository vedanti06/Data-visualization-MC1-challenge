TITLE: UPGRADES NEEDED FOR ABILA'S DEEP WATER PORT 

PUBLISHED: 1993/09/20
 

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

As part of the government brokered deal with GAStech International, Abila must make upgrades to their deep water port. Additionally, the Port will need to dredge the port killing two birds with one stone: increasing the depth of the port and providing fill to raise the East Pier.
