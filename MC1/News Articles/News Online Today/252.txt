TITLE: Annual POK Rally Ends in Riot, Again 

PUBLISHED: 2013/06/20
 
LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

POK members held their annual demonstration in Abila yesterday.   

Police and government security forces set up between the protestors and the Capitol building in a show of force, hoping to prevent the kind of chaos that occurred during last year's riot.
