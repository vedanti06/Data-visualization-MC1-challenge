TITLE: MINISTER OF HEALTH TO PROPOSE SOLUTION TO TOWN POISONING 

PUBLISHED: 1999/07/08

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

Prime Minister of Health Cesare Nespola announced he would sponsor a bill to increase taxes on foreign energy development in Kronos intended to fund environmental cleanup and improved healthcare for rural towns.  Following a series of meetings with members of the activist group Protectors of Kronos (POK) the announcement was made last week.
