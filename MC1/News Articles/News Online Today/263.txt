TITLE: TEN-YEAR OLD ELODIS GIRL DIES OF POISONING 

PUBLISHED: 1998/08/20
 
LOCATION: ELODIS, Kronos 

Ten-year old Juliana Vann died yesterday afternoon, surrounded by her family and friends.  After visits to many hospitals, a doctor associated with the international assistance organization Wellness For All recommended Juliana be tests for benzene poisoning.
