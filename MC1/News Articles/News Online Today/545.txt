TITLE: POSSIBLE CONTAMINATION IN ELODIS  

PUBLISHED: 1997/04/24
 

LOCATION: ELODIS, Kronos 

About 25 km West of Abila is the small rural township of Elodis, situated on the Tiskele River. Roughly 20 km upstream is the Tiskele Bend site operated by GAStech International on government-owned land.
