TITLE: Ministry of Health Investigates Contamination Complaints 

PUBLISHED: 1998/11/10
 
LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

Minister of Health Cesare Nespola announced today that his organization would be investigating claims of abnormal health issues in areas surrounding the Tiskele Bend drill site.  However, I believe it is important as Minister of Health to do due diligence and investigate the reports of sickness near the Elodis township.
