TITLE: Minister of Health Dies Suddenly 

PUBLISHED: 2001/06/15
 

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

ABILA, Kronos - It is with great sorrow that we announce the death of Minister of Health Cesare Nespola. 

Shortly after hearing of Nespola's death, President Kapelou announced that his nephew Vincent Kapelou would serve as his administration's new Minister of Health.
