TITLE: POK REPRESENTATIVES MEET WITH KRONOS MINISTER OF HEALTH 

PUBLISHED: 1998/11/15

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

Representatives from the social activist group Protectors of Kronos (POK) met earlier this week with Cesare Nespola, Minister of Health to discuss resolutions to the water contamination that has plagued Elodis since the late 1990s.
