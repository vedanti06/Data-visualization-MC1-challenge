TITLE: Police Hold News Conference on GAStech Kidnapping 

PUBLISHED: 2014/01/21
 

LOCATION: ABILA, Kronos 

The Abila police held a press conference at nine this morning on the kidnapping GAStech headquarters.  Police informed reporters that four of the GAStech employees whose whereabouts were unconfirmed yesterday were found overnight, leaving ten people who are presumed missing.
